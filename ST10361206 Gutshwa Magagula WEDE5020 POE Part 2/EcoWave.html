Welcome to EcoWave

EcoWave is dedicated to combating plastic pollution in our oceans a problem that has persisted longer than expected. Our website serves to encourage, empower, and unite people in taking care of our oceans when given the opportunity.

We are a dedicated non-profit organization committed to protecting our oceans from the devastating effects of plastic pollution. Our mission is to inspire and empower the younger generation to take proactive steps in preserving marine life and ensuring a healthier environment for future generations.

By raising awareness, organizing clean-up campaigns, and providing educational resources, EcoWave aims to make a lasting impact on the fight against ocean pollution, fostering a world where the beauty and vitality of our oceans are preserved for all.
